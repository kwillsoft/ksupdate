The Product

OncoBulb is a RESTful LAMP-stack service built as an add-on for the National Center for Biotechnology Information's public-facing API. This application allows researchers,teachers, and students to select one of the 13 most prevalent cancer types in the U.S. and instantly access up-to-the-minute peer-reviewed data regarding plant-based approaches to potential treatments.
