The Product

FoodEye is a custom WordPress plugin initially developed for a client in the restaurant industry (currently for sale). The client (restaurant owner) needed a convenient way to:

  • Be able to archive all of her menu item ingredients,
  • Allow her employees to have independent access to the documentation, allowing questions to be answered whether or not there was a chef readily available.
  • Make certain frequently needed fields readily accessible (i.e. meat content, spice level, known allergens, & optimum beverage pairing)
  • Restrict the user permissions by role
